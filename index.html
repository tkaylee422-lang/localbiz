PHO 101

Simply The Best Authentic Vietnamese Pho

A Tradition of Excellence

For over 35 years, the Tran family has been creating traditional pho bowls and authentic Vietnamese dishes using secret family recipes passed down for three generations. We pride ourselves on using only fresh, high quality, authentic ingredients, sourcing our fruit, vegetables, and meat locally wherever possible.

Pho was born in Northern Vietnam during the mid-1880s and has evolved into the beloved national dish. Our family-owned restaurant welcomes families of all sizes to enjoy our generous portions at very reasonable prices. Everyone is sure to find something they love!
